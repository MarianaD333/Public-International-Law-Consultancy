Our Services

Strategic Legal Advisory

We deliver comprehensive legal guidance on core public international law frameworks, helping states and sovereign entities confidently navigate their rights and international obligations. Our assessments help mitigate geopolitical and compliance risks before they emerge on the global stage.

International Dispute Resolution

Our consultants provide tactical analysis and support for complex proceedings before the International Court of Justice (ICJ), the Permanent Court of Arbitration (PCA), and specialized international tribunals. We assist counsel teams with rigorous evidentiary compilation and memorial drafting.

Treaty Drafting & Negotiations

We support government delegations during bilateral and multilateral negotiations. Our services cover structural drafting and textual interpretations of treaties, border accords, and investment agreements to safeguard domestic sovereign interests while aligning with global rules.

Global Experience

Our mission is to empower states and communities around the world to be able to use the tools of international law.

Our international legal work has involved the following countries: Bangladesh, Egypt, Ghana, Indonesia, Kosovo, Libya, Morocco, Pakistan, Palestine, Rwanda, United Kingdom, Saudi Arabia, Syria, United States.
